# Soul Garden App - Paid Media Strategy

## Objective
Drive brand awareness for Soul Garden's AI-powered manifestation experience, funnel traffic to GodGPT social channels (TikTok, Instagram), and increase app installs through strategic paid media campaigns targeting mindfulness and personal growth audiences.

---

## Channels

**Primary Channels (70% budget)**
- **TikTok Ads** - Native video format ideal for showcasing plant growth animations; strongest engagement with 18-35 wellness audience
- **Instagram Ads** - Multi-format (Stories/Reels/Feed) targeting mindfulness communities; high visual impact for plant aesthetics

**Secondary Channels (20% budget)**
- **Pinterest Ads** - High-intent users actively searching wellness/manifestation content; longer consideration window

**Testing Channels (10% budget)**
- **YouTube Shorts Ads** - Complementary short-form video reach; optimize based on Month 1 performance

---

## KPIs

**Awareness Tier**
- Impressions: 5M+ (Month 1-3 cumulative)
- Video Views: 2M+ (3-second views)
- Reach: 1M+ unique users

**Engagement Tier**
- Social Profile Visits: 50K+
- Click-Through Rate (CTR): 2.5%+ (TikTok/IG), 1.5%+ (Pinterest)
- Social Engagement Rate: 5%+ (likes, comments, shares)

**Conversion Tier**
- App Installs: 15K+ (across 3 months)
- Cost Per Install (CPI): <$3.50 (TikTok/IG), <$5 (Pinterest)
- Install-to-Registration Rate: 40%+
- Day 7 Retention: 25%+

---

## 3-Month Campaign Plan

### Month 1: Discovery & Awareness
**Theme:** "What if your wishes could grow?"

**Focus:** Brand introduction, creative/audience testing, drive curiosity

**Tactics:**
- Launch 8-10 creative variants (UGC-style, product demos, emotional hooks)
- Test 5 audience segments (manifestation enthusiasts, meditation users, mental wellness, plant lovers, spirituality)
- Heavy TikTok/IG focus with 9:16 short videos (15-30s)
- Soft CTA: "See how it works" → Social profile visit
- Implement pixel tracking and build custom audiences

**Creative Priorities:** 
- Plant growth timelapse (seed → bloom in 15s)
- User POV: morning ritual with affirmations
- Emotional transformation storytelling

---

### Month 2: Social Proof & Engagement
**Theme:** "Join 10K+ people growing their dreams"

**Focus:** Leverage Month 1 learners, amplify social proof, drive deeper engagement

**Tactics:**
- Scale top 3 performing creatives from Month 1
- Introduce user testimonial videos (before/after emotional states)
- Launch Lookalike Audiences (1-3%) based on converters
- Add Pinterest campaigns targeting "manifestation journal," "daily affirmations," "mindfulness app" keywords
- Medium CTA: "Start your garden" → Direct to social posts with app link
- Implement retargeting for social profile visitors

**Creative Priorities:**
- User success stories (real people + their blooming plants)
- Side-by-side comparison (Day 1 vs Day 30)
- AI personalization highlight (show custom affirmations)

---

### Month 3: Conversion & Activation
**Theme:** "30-Day Manifestation Challenge"

**Focus:** Drive installs, activate users, build habit loop

**Tactics:**
- Launch campaign around "30-Day Challenge" (if applicable, or "Start Your Journey")
- Heavy retargeting on social engagers and profile visitors
- Introduce limited-time incentive (e.g., "Unlock premium seed pack")
- Shift 40% budget to App Install campaigns (vs. traffic)
- Add conversion campaigns on TikTok/IG with app install objective
- Strong CTA: "Download now" → Direct to App Store/Google Play

**Creative Priorities:**
- Challenge teaser (rapid plant growth montage)
- Day-in-the-life: 3-minute morning routine
- Results-focused: "My anxiety plant bloomed after 21 days"
- Urgency messaging (if time-bound offer exists)

## Creative Messaging

### Core Message Pillars

**1. Transformation**
- "Watch your anxiety transform into peace, one petal at a time"
- "Your feelings deserve to bloom"
- Emotional arc: stressed → calm, anxious → grounded

**2. Daily Ritual**
- "3 minutes a day to water your dreams"
- "Morning affirmations that actually feel personal"
- Simplicity + consistency = habit formation

**3. Visual Magic**
- "Your wishes become beautiful, living art"
- "AI-animated plants that grow as you grow"
- Stunning plant animations as hero visual

**4. AI Personalization**
- "Affirmations written just for you by AI"
- "Every plant is unique to your journey"
- Emphasize custom experience, not generic quotes

### Creative Formats

- **9:16 Short Videos (15-30s):** Plant growth timelapse, user testimonials, POV morning routine
- **Static Carousels (3-5 slides):** Before/after emotional states, app feature walkthrough, affirmation examples
- **UGC-Style Videos:** Real users sharing their plants + progress
- **Text Animation:** Beautiful affirmation quotes on botanical backgrounds

### Hook Examples (First 3 Seconds)
- "I planted my anxiety in an app..."
- "This AI knows exactly what I need to hear"
- "30 days ago, I planted a seed..."
- "What if manifestation was this beautiful?"

---

## Tracking & Optimization

**Weekly Reviews:** 
- Adjust bids/budgets based on CPI and CTR
- Pause underperforming creatives (<1.5% CTR)
- Test new ad copy variants

**Audience Refinement:**
- Build custom audiences from: video viewers (50%+), social engagers, app installers
- Create Lookalikes by Month 2
- Exclude converters to reduce wasted spend

**Attribution Setup:**
- UTM parameters for all ads (source, medium, campaign, content)
- App attribution via Adjust/AppsFlyer or native TikTok/Meta SDKs
- Track full funnel: Ad View → Profile Visit → App Install → Registration → Day 1/7/30 Retention

---

## Success Criteria (End of Month 3)

✅ 15K+ app installs at <$3.50 CPI  
✅ 50K+ social profile visits from paid traffic  
✅ 2M+ video views across platforms  
✅ 40%+ install-to-registration rate  
✅ Positive ROAS if monetization exists (track LTV)
