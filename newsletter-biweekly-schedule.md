# Soul Garden Bi-Weekly Newsletter Schedule
## 6-Month Campaign | Subscriber Growth & LTV Optimization

---

## Campaign Overview

### Primary Goals
1. **Drive Subscriber Intent**: Convert 15-20% of engaged free users to paid subscribers
2. **Increase LTV**: Drive repeat engagement and reduce churn by 25%
3. **New Product Awareness**: Build anticipation for upcoming feature launches

### Target Audience
- **Primary**: Gen Z women (18-28), current free/paid users
- **Psychographics**: "That girl" lifestyle enthusiasts, manifestation practitioners, self-growth seekers
- **Engagement level**: Email opens 40%+, active app users (7-day window)

### Timeline
**6 months | 12 bi-weekly newsletters**
- Phase 1 (Weeks 1-8): Awareness & Foundation
- Phase 2 (Weeks 9-16): Engagement & Transformation
- Phase 3 (Weeks 17-24): Community & Expansion

---

## Budget Overview

### Total Campaign Budget: $15,000 (6 months)

**Monthly Breakdown**: $2,500/month

**Per Newsletter Allocation**: ~$1,250

- **Paid Ads**: $500 (40%)
  - Meta Ads (Instagram/Facebook): $300
  - TikTok Ads: $150

  - ESP costs (Klaviyo/Mailchimp advanced)
  - Segmentation & automation

- **Content & Design**: $250 (20%)
  - Email design/coding
  - Static graphics (4-6 per newsletter)
  - Copywriting optimization
- **Testing & Optimization**: $125 (10%)
  - Landing page tools
  - Analytics/tracking
  - Conversion optimization

### Paid Ad Strategy
**Objective**: Drive email signups → newsletter engagement → conversion

**Funnel**:
1. **Awareness**: Instagram Stories/Reels (carousel ads) → Landing page
2. **Engagement**: Email newsletter → In-app conversion
3. **Retargeting**: TikTok/Meta retargeting (engaged non-converters)

**Creative Format**:
- 3-slide carousel: Problem → Solution → CTA
- Video (15-30s): User testimonial or founder message
- Static: High-impact quote + visual

## Asset Design Specifications

### Email Template Standards

#### Layout Structure:
- **Width**: 600px (mobile-responsive)
- **Padding**: 24px horizontal, 32px vertical
- **Header**: 120px height (logo + navigation)
- **Hero Section**: 400px height (image + headline)
- **Body**: Modular blocks (stackable on mobile)
- **CTA Button**: 52px height, 200px width, 12px border radius
- **Footer**: 80px height (social links + unsubscribe)

#### Typography:
- **Headline (H1)**: 32px, bold (700), line-height 1.2
- **Subheadline (H2)**: 24px, semibold (600), line-height 1.3
- **Body Text**: 16px, regular (400), line-height 1.6
- **CTA Text**: 18px, semibold (600)
- **Caption/Footnote**: 14px, regular (400)

#### Color Palette:
- **Primary**: #FE5706 (orange) - CTA buttons, links, accents
- **Secondary**: #FFA147 (soft orange) - highlights, secondary CTAs
- **Background**: #FAFAFA (off-white) - email body
- **Surface**: #FFFFFF (white) - content cards
- **Text Primary**: #1A1A1A (near-black) - headlines, body
- **Text Secondary**: #666666 (gray) - captions, metadata
- **Border**: #E0E0E0 (light gray) - dividers, card borders

#### Button States:
- **Default**: Background #FE5706, text #FFFFFF
- **Hover**: Background #E54E06 (darker orange)
- **Pressed**: Opacity 0.85, scale 0.98
- **Shadow**: 0 4px 12px rgba(254, 87, 6, 0.3)

### Social Still Specifications

#### Instagram Feed (1:1)
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1080px
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Text**: Maximum 3 lines, 40-50 characters per line
- **Font**: Space Grotesk (primary), 48px headline, 24px body
- **Branding**: Logo in corner (80x80px)
- **Color Overlay**: Gradient or solid (brand colors)

#### Instagram Story (9:16)
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1920px
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or MP4 (video)
- **Safe Zone**: 120px from top/bottom (avoid UI overlap)
- **Text**: Maximum 2 lines, 30 characters per line
- **CTA**: Swipe-up link or sticker (if available)
- **Interactivity**: Polls, question boxes, countdowns

#### TikTok (9:16)
- **Dimensions**: 1080x1920px
- **Format**: MP4 (video) or JPG (still)
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds (video)
- **Text Overlay**: Large, bold, high-contrast
- **Captions**: Always include (accessibility + watch without sound)
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds must grab attention

#### Pinterest (2:3)
- **Dimensions**: 1000x1500px
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Text**: Headline + 3-5 supporting points
- **Design**: Vertical flow, scannable
- **Branding**: Logo at top or bottom
- **CTA**: "Learn More" or "Try Now" button graphic

### Copy Guidelines

#### Email Subject Lines:
- **Length**: 40-50 characters (mobile preview)
- **Tone**: Conversational, Gen Z-friendly, emoji-friendly (max 1-2 emojis)
- **Personalization**: Use first name if available
- **Avoid**: Clickbait, all caps, excessive punctuation
- **Test**: Always A/B test 2 variations

#### Preheaders:
- **Length**: 80-100 characters
- **Function**: Expand on subject line (not repeat it)
- **Tone**: Supportive, benefit-driven

#### Body Copy:
- **Sentence Length**: 15-20 words max
- **Paragraph Length**: 2-3 sentences max
- **Tone**: Friendly, empowering, direct
- **Voice**: "We're in this together" (not preachy)
- **Calls-to-Action**: Action verbs ("Start," "Create," "Join," "Unlock")

#### Button Copy:
- **Length**: 2-4 words
- **Action-Oriented**: "Start Your Trial," "Create Your Circle"
- **Avoid**: "Click here," "Learn more" (vague)
